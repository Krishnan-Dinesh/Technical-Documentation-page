Google Pixel 6 Summary

Google Pixel 6 mobile was launched on 19th October 2021. The phone comes with a 6.40-inch touchscreen display with a punch-hole at the top where the camera resides. Google Pixel 6 is powered by a 2.8GHz octa-core Google Tensor processor that features 2 cores clocked at 2.8GHz and 2 cores clocked at 2.25GHz. It comes with 8GB of RAM. The Google Pixel 6 runs Android 12 and is powered by a 4614mAh battery. The Google Pixel 6 supports wireless charging, as well as proprietary fast charging.

Google Pixel 6 Full Specifications

Display:

The Google Pixel 6 comes with a 6.40 inch display with 90Hz refresh rate. This has a resolution of 1080x2400 pixels at a pixel density of 411 pixels per inch (ppi) and an aspect ratio of 20:9.

Hardware:

Google Pixel 6 is based on Android 12 and packs 128GB of inbuilt storage. The Google Pixel 6 is a dual-SIM (GSM and GSM) mobile that accepts Nano-SIM and eSIM cards. The Google Pixel 6 measures 158.60 x 74.80 x 8.90mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 207.00 grams. It was launched in Kinda Coral, Sorta Seafoam, and Stormy Black colours. It features an IP68 rating for dust and water protection.

Camera:

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Google Pixel 6 on the rear packs a dual camera setup featuring a 50-megapixel primary camera with an f/1.85 aperture and a pixel size of 1.2-micron, and a 12-megapixel camera with an f/2.2 aperture and a pixel size of 1.25-micron. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It has a single front camera setup for selfies, featuring an 8-megapixel sensor with an f/2.0 aperture and a pixel size of 1.12-micron.

Connectivity:

Connectivity options on the Google Pixel 6 include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/ax, GPS, Bluetooth v5.20, NFC, USB Type-C, 3G, and 4G (with support for Band 40 used by some LTE networks in India) with active 4G on both SIM cards.

Sensors:

Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, barometer, compass/ magnetometer, gyroscope, in-display fingerprint sensor, proximity sensor, and fingerprint sensor.
